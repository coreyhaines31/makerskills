## How to use during drafting

1. Pick 1–2 accounts whose audience or angle aligns with the property being promoted
2. Pull their last 5–10 posts (agent-browser if auth-friendly, else ask Corey to paste examples)
3. Extract: **hook openers**, **structural patterns** (numbered list, contrarian-take, story open), **CTA styles**, **post length**, **line-break rhythm**
4. Choose one pattern that fits the property + content type
5. Apply the pattern to Corey's voice (don't mimic their voice — only the structure)

## Patterns library

As patterns get extracted, log them in `patterns.md` (created on first scan) so they accumulate over time and the skill gets sharper with use.

## Notes

- LinkedIn often blocks unauthenticated scraping. If agent-browser walls, fall back to: pasting examples, pulling from their public newsletters, or scraping their X accounts.
- Never copy phrasing — only structure, format, hook archetype.
- Inspiration is a starting point. Corey's voice rules in `voice.md` always override format choices.
